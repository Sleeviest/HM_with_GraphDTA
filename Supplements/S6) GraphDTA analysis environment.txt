Supplementary Methods S1. Computational Environment

All preprocessing, network construction, and post-processing analyses were conducted in Python within a Jupyter Notebook environment.

Hardware:
- CPU: AMD Ryzen 7 9800X3D
- GPU: NVIDIA RTX 5080
- RAM: 32 GB

Software:
- OS: Windows 11
- Python: 3.12.10
- PyTorch: 2.12.0.dev20260223+cu128
- PyTorch Geometric: 2.7.0
- RDKit: 2025.09.5
- pandas: 3.0.1
- numpy: 2.3.5
- Cytoscape: 3.10.3

GraphDTA training was performed using the GINConvNet architecture on the Davis benchmark dataset. The training set was split into training and validation subsets at an 8:2 ratio with a fixed random seed of 42. Early stopping with patience = 30 was applied. The best model was obtained at epoch 155, and training terminated at epoch 185.
